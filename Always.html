Always

A collection of heartfelt poetry celebrating our eternal love.

Timeless Embrace

Your eyes, a universe of stars,
Your smile, the sun's warm embrace.
In your love, I've found my home,
A timeless, endless space.
With every breath, every heartbeat,
Our love grows, pure and sweet.

Seasons of Us

Through seasons changing, years unfold,
Our love remains, forever bold.
Hand in hand, hearts entwined,
A story of love, uniquely designed.
Spring's renewal, summer's glow,
Autumn's warmth, winter's snow.

Silent Symphony

In quiet moments, loud crowds,
Our love speaks without a sound.
A language of souls, pure and true,
Every day, my heart chooses you.
Unspoken words, tender touch,
In your presence, I find so much.

Unwritten Chapters

Like pages of a book unread,
Our future waits, adventures spread.
With you beside me, come what may,
Our love grows stronger every day.
New horizons, dreams to chase,
Together we'll find our perfect place.

Eternal Glow

In laughter, tears, and quiet nights,
Our love shines bright, reaches new heights.
Forever and always, this I know,
With you, my heart will always glow.
A flame eternal, burning strong,
To you, my love, I'll always belong.
